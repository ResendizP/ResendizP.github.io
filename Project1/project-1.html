Yeseva One / Regular

This interesting font is feminine essence of the whole world. It was updated multiple times and was last updated in September 2012. The latest version of this typeface is 2.0. This attractive and fantastic font was established by Jovanny Lemonad.The last version of this typeface has new characters. Its unique characteristics are many benefits in the design field for different purposes. This font has OTF and TTF formats that are suitable for personal-type projects. It consists of 3 weights and 10 styles that are useful in the design industry. It gained a good position in the font world due to its unique and eye-catching appearance. This font has many closest fonts that are perfect for pairing with it for various official purposes such as Meji font and backslash font. You can use this typeface for tags, postcard designs, t-shirt designs, and photography purposes. This font is available for use on social media sites, wall murals designs, and ebook covers designs.
