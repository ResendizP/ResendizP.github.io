Find the indoor palm that best suits your needs

Aesthetic

Sago Palms are anything but basic. If you want to bring an exotic and eclectic feel to a room. Sago Palms will surely get the job done. And can you believe these funky plants date back to prehistoric times?! That’s right, these plants existed back when dinosaurs roamed the earth!
These incredibly hardy houseplants thrive wherever they are planted, and the large feathery foliage adds so much flare and style to a home.
If you want the calm, tropical energy of a palm tree but just don’t have the space, the Parlor Palm is the perfect option for you! As one of the only palms that are the perfect size to grow on a table or desk, these sweet little plants will radiate good vibes without overwhelming a space.
Parlor Palms are great additions to terrariums and small container gardens! The sweet little trees are also safe for pets and children!

Elegance

When you think of palm trees, it’s likely that the first thing that comes to mind is your typical island scene, three large fanning leaves, and a couple of coconuts. But when it comes to indoor palms, the variety of colors, shapes, and sizes is vast!
No matter your vibe, palms have a way of just taking your indoor home décor to the next level. Turn your living space into an exotic oasis when you incorporate these Palm Trees into your space! As one of the most graceful and elegant palms on the market, there’s really no reason why you wouldn’t want the beautiful Date Palm growing in your indoor space. These slow growers have graceful arching fronds and an upright growth habit.
The foliage on these sweet trees is similar to bamboo but more lacey and delicate. Pot one of these show-stoppers up and watch as it lights up a space!

Low maintenance

Ponytail Palms are easily the life of the party and the spunkiest member of the palm tree family. Curly green strands of foliage explode from the woody trunk of these tropical trees and make for a grand statement in any indoor room!
As an added bonus to their unique appearance, Ponytail Palms boast an extremely low-maintenance care regimen. The trees draw a close relation to the succulent family, as they are extremely drought tolerant and require minimal watering to stay happy. Ponytail Palms, also enjoy bright indirect sunlight and a well-draining soil medium.
